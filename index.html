Best of Place

Kingfisher Retreat
HOTEL
Kingfisher Hotel is a 5-star hotel that offers a luxurious stay on the banks of Al Ain River. The hotel features spacious rooms and suites with balconies overlooking the river or the city. The hotel also offers a range of facilities and services, including: Outdoor pool Spa Fitness center Restaurant serving international cuisine Rooftop bar
Al Faya Retreat
HOTEL
Al faya retreat is a 4-star boutique hotel that offers a unique stay in the heart of the Liwa Desert. The hotel features luxurious tents equipped with all modern comforts, including private bathrooms, air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities, such as: Camel riding Desert safaris Sandboarding Stargazing Bedouin parties
Al Rayaheen
HOTEL
Nestled in the Khorfakkan valley, Al Rayaheen Retreat offers a unique stay in beautifully restored heritage houses. Each house features a variety of spaces, including bedrooms, a living room, a traditional liwan area, and outdoor seating, allowing you to experience Emirati culture firsthand.
Al Badayer Retreat
HOTEL
Escape to the tranquility of the desert at Al Badayer Retreat, a luxurious eco-retreat nestled amidst the majestic dunes of the Empty Quarter. Relax in spacious villas with private pools, offering breathtaking desert views
